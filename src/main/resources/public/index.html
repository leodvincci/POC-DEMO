About

Our App is a tool that students can use to help them be a success with their academic goals. Students plan for future courses by saving it, with the help of a course feedback and rating system. This rating system allows other users who have already taken the class to “rate” the class through likes and dislikes. This allows the student to navigate which class they may want to take, and which not to take.

Our purpose is to create an interactive site for DePaul Students. The goal is to allow students to leave reviews on classes, so that the whole DePaul community has more guidance on classes.

We would be happy to have your feedback on this site. Thanks!
